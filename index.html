About Us!
Hi, Welcome To Event Management System
Event Management System - Powered by Riviera, VIT University(2021)

Events are organised primarily by the students of the VIT Vellore. It has a wide variety of competitions and events, which range from dramatic, literary, musical, dance, debate events all the way to informal events.

The cultural events has come a long way from its humble beginnings. It was first introduced by the VIT Chancellor, Dr. G. Viswanathan. The first edition of Riviera was in the year 2002 and was a success with a wide range of events in all cultural spheres including music, dance, drama, literary events and more. Sports competitions were also part of this first festival. It saw a fair amount of external participants which would grow in the years to come.It would also be included on the list of the Top 10 Cultural Festivals in India.Started to provide an alternative to the students from strenuous academics, it evolved into something that would draw in crowds from most colleges and schools around the region. The students were meant to gain basic organizational experience to prepare them for similar situations in the real world. The festival grew by immense proportions, from having a budget of a few lakhs to a few crores for instance, that the students had to keep raising the bar to meet the different challenges with each new Riviera.
